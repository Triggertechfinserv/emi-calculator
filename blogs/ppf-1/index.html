What is a Public Provident Fund (PPF)?

The Public Provident Fund (PPF) is one of India‚Äôs most trusted long-term savings and investment schemes, backed by the Government of India. Introduced in 1968 under the PPF Act, it offers investors a safe avenue to grow their money while enjoying tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.
