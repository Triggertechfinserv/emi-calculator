What is EMI?

Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is the fixed amount you pay each month towards a loan. Every EMI contains two parts — interest and principal. In the early months, the interest portion is higher; over time, principal share increases.

Formula: EMI = P × r × (1+r)n / [(1+r)n − 1], where P = principal, r = monthly rate (annual%/12/100), n = number of months.
